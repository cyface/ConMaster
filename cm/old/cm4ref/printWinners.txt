  `Procedure: pPrintWinners
  `Tim White
  `10/03/94

  `This Procedure prints a list of the winners of all events

QUERY([Person Section];[Person Section]Section Full Code=False)
ORDER BY([Person Section];[Person Section]Registration Type;>;[Person]Last Name;>;[Person]First Name;>)
OUTPUT FORM([Person Section];"Winners List.p")
PRINT SELECTION([Person Section])
OUTPUT FORM([Person Section];"Output.w")
